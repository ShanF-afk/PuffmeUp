Products

Vape Juice

When heated by the vape device, the liquid turns into vapor, which is then inhaled. Vape juice comes in many flavors and nicotine levels, making it customizable for users.

Dispossable Vape

A disposable vape is a compact, pre-filled vaping device designed for single use. It comes with a built-in battery and a pre-loaded amount of vape juice, eliminating the need for refilling or recharging. Once the vape is empty, it is discarded and replaced with a new one.

Mechanical Vape

A mechanical vape mod (or mech mod) is a unregulated vaping device that delivers power directly from the battery to the atomizer without any circuit boards, safety features, or electronic regulations.
